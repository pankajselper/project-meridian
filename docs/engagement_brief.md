\# Project Meridian — Engagement Brief



\*\*Client (simulated):\*\* NorthBridge \& Co. — a UK-headquartered online gift, homeware, and lifestyle retailer trading since 2009, generating most of its revenue from the UK and continental Europe, with a long tail of orders across 40+ countries.



\*\*Context:\*\* After a decade of organic growth, NorthBridge's board has approved a strategic review of two growth markets — the UAE (as a GCC/re-export hub) and India (a large, fast-digitizing D2C market) — but wants this growth funded by fixing inefficiencies in the existing business first, not purely by new capital. The CFO is also concerned that revenue booked in AED or INR introduces currency risk the company has never had to manage.



\*\*Mandate:\*\* Produce an end-to-end data diagnostic of NorthBridge's customer base and revenue engine, quantify where money is leaking today, build forward-looking models of customer value and risk, and translate it all into a board-ready expansion recommendation — including the currency risk the company would be taking on.



\*\*Key questions this engagement must answer:\*\*

1\. How healthy is the current customer base — retention, concentration, who's about to churn?

2\. Where is revenue leaking today (discounts, returns, cancellations), and is it worth fixing before expanding?

3\. Which customer/product behaviours should inform the UAE/India go-to-market plan?

4\. What does FX exposure look like under a UAE/India expansion — and is hedging worth the cost?

5\. What should NorthBridge do in the next 90 days?



\*\*Deliverables expected:\*\* a clean analytics data warehouse, three audience-specific Power BI dashboards, predictive models (churn, CLV, forecast), an interactive scenario simulator, and a board-ready presentation with a one-page business case.
